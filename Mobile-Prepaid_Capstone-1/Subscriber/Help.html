Frequently Asked Questions

Mobile Prepaid is our pay-as-you-go service that gives you full control over your mobile spending. You add credit to your account before using our services, which means no contracts, no credit checks, and no unexpected bills.

You can recharge your account through our mobile app, website, at any of our retail partners, or by purchasing a recharge card. Simply enter your phone number and the recharge code or select your preferred payment method.

You can check your balance by dialing *123# from your mobile phone, logging into your account on our website or mobile app, or by sending "BAL" to 1234 via SMS.

When your credit runs out, you won't be able to make calls, send texts, or use data until you recharge. However, you'll still be able to receive calls and texts for a period of time, and you can always call emergency services regardless of your balance.

You can change your prepaid plan anytime by logging into your account on our website or mobile app, selecting "My Plan," and choosing a new plan from the available options. The change will take effect immediately or at the end of your current billing cycle.
